Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a kind-hearted high schooler who becomes involved with the world of curses and jujutsu sorcerers after swallowing a finger of Sukuna, a powerful curse. To protect the world from curses and to save Yuji from execution due to being Sukuna’s vessel, Yuji joins Tokyo Jujutsu High School where he trains to fight curses. He is mentored by the powerful sorcerer Satoru Gojo and forms strong bonds with fellow students Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki. Together, they face vicious curses, deadly missions, and uncover dark secrets involving curses and sorcerers. Their goal is to eliminate curses and prevent the resurgence of Sukuna’s full power while navigating complex alliances and battles

Attack on Titan is set in a world where humanity lives within massive walls to protect themselves from Titans—giant humanoid creatures that devour humans. Eren Yeager, after witnessing his mother’s death during a Titan attack, joins the military to fight these creatures. Throughout the series, Eren and his friends uncover the mysteries of the Titans and the political intrigue behind the walls. Eren gains the ability to transform into a Titan, becoming a pivotal figure in the battle between humans and Titans. The story explores themes of freedom, sacrifice, and the harsh realities of war, culminating in an epic conflict involving humanity’s survival and the truth about the Titans and their origins
